Research Interests

I am interested in searching for signatures of non-gravitational interactions between dark matter particles and Standard Model (SM) states through both direct and indirect detection methods leveraging signatures from astrophysical objects. Additionally, I am also working on other BSM scenarios like axion-like particles and neutrino non-standard interactions in astrophysical environments including supernovae and white dwarfs. I am also interested in exploring the model-building aspects of these BSM scenarios to understand the unexplained mysteries of our Universe.
