Research Interests

I work on dark matter phenomenology and particle astrophysics. My research uses celestial objects — neutron stars, white dwarfs, brown dwarfs, and the first generation of stars — as natural laboratories for dark matter, connecting particle models to signals in neutrino telescopes, gravitational-wave observatories, and direct-detection experiments.
